Not a bar.
Not a network.
A home base.

Velvet Social is a community-first social club for Black, queer folx — built around the idea that we deserve spaces that hold all of us.

Not just the pride parade version. Not just the cookout version. The soft, the edgy, the weird, the regal, the tired, the thriving — all of it, all at once.

Because Black queer identity has never been one thing. Neither are we.

The Vision
"Community for the whole you — not just the part that's easy to explain."
The Aesthetic
Luxe but yours. Grown and sexy but never intimidating. No rainbow chaos.
The Promise
A space where you can Show Out, Slow Down, and Show Up — all in the same breath.

We're building
something for you.

Be the first to know when Velvet Social opens its doors. No spam. Just us, when we're ready.
